Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you register, along with the expense records you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information, such as browser type and access times, to help keep the Service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely to operate and improve Spendly: authenticating your account, displaying your expense history, generating category and spending summaries, and troubleshooting issues. We do not use your expense data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting or font delivery services) to operate the Service. These providers process data only to the extent necessary to provide their service and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, or if you'd like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page.
